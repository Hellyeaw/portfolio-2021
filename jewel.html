Sara's Garden

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

"Sara's Garden" is a local florist, run by a mother as a hobby. She mostly sells plants suitable for apartments. In her logo she wanted to imply the feeling of energy, livelihood and colorfulness to remind her audience of her beautiful, alive and fresh plants. Her goals were to expand her business, attract more mothers and young adults in her neighborhood and to stand out among her competitors.
