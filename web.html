Sending Youth to War

Wake Me Up is an experimentation of the developers' vision towards art, music, narration and gameplay. This demo was created during the Game Endeavor game jam. This prototype was a part of the pre-production stage of a bigger more complex project called SemiColon. The ultimate aim of the project is to raise awareness on mental health issues, and through this prototype we were able to test some of our game mechanic ideas.
